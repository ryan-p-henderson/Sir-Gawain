Welcome to the project site for Gawain's Ghost: Examination of Cultural Allusions in Sir Gawain and the Green Knight. This project is a part of the University of Pittsburgh Computational Methods in Humanities course, by senior Ryan Henderson and junior Phil Ferguson. The aim of the project is to determine authorial opinion on various cultures by finding where cultural allusions are made and by whom. Use the tabs above to learn about our research question and methodology, explore the text, and see project results.
